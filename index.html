What is NIFTy?

Nanke Invitational Forensics Tournament, or NIFTy, is an annual event held by IBST.

Due to COVID 19 restrictions in the past few years, NIFTy was not held for the past two years.

This year, NIFTy is back with events such as impromptu and policy debate this year.

NIFTy has been an annual tradition at IBST and we welcome you to join!
