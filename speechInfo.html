Forensic Speech Event Descriptions




(1) Dramatic Interpretation: In this event, participants will have to give a 7-10 minute performance based on a memorized speech/script/scene from any source of media. The speaker will have to memorize and physically act out the script. Performances must also include an introduction written by the student to contextualize the performance and state the title and author.

No props are allowed, so if the script requires an object, the speaker must act like it is there.

The performance will be graded on:
Delivery: Did the speaker have to deliver the speech smoothly? Did their voice sound confident, and did they have appropriate volume?
Stage presence: Did the speaker maintain eye contact? Did they face the audience? Did they use movement to convey emotion?
