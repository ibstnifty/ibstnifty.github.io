Debate Events

Policy Division:

I. Rules & Guidelines:

  1. Each team will consist of 2 debaters, with one team on the Affirmative side and one team on the Negative side. The speech times are listed below accordingly in the “Format” section.

  2. Since this is a US-style event, teams are allowed to bring prepared cases, either printed cases or electronic copies on devices are permitted.

  3. Searching for additional evidence on the internet while the debate round is in session is not allowed and will count as cheating.

  4. Speed-talking through a speech is not allowed and speaker points will be taken off for doing so. Please speak at an intelligible pace for the judges and the opponents to be able to understand the contents of the speech.

  5. There will be one judge per preliminary round and a panel of three judges for each elimination round, including the final round.

  6. Please be respectful to other debaters and your judge. If any participants feel that they have been disrespected or bullied, please bring up your concerns to the tournament organizers.

  7. Debaters will be awarded speaker awards and finalist awards on top of participation awards so please don't be discouraged if your team loses some debate rounds.

II. Format:

  1. Speeches:
    1. Each team has 8 minutes of prep time during the round itself.
    2. Each team has 2 constructive speeches and 2 rebuttal speeches. The speaking order for Policy debate is as follows:
    Order Speech Time
    1 1st Affirmative Constructive Speech 8 minutes
    2 Negative Cross-X of Affirmative 3 minutes
    3 1st Negative Constructive Speech 8 minutes
    4 Affirmative Cross-X of Negative 3 minutes
    5 2nd Affirmative Constructive Speech 8 minutes
    6 Negative Cross-X of Affirmative 3 minutes
    7 2nd Negative Constructive Speech 8 minutes
    8 Affirmative Cross-X of Negative 3 minutes
    9 1st Negative Rebuttal Speech 5 minutes
    10 1st Affirmative Rebuttal Speech 5 minutes
    11 2nd Negative Rebuttal Speech 5 minutes
    12 2nd Affirmative Rebuttal Speech 5 minutes
  2. Rounds Set-Up:
    1. As of now, there will be 4 preliminary rounds and the results of these rounds will determine which teams break (in other words, advance to the elimination rounds and final rounds).
    2. The elimination rounds include the Octo-Finals, Quarter-Finals, and Semi-Finals. The top 2 teams will then advance to the Grand Finals.

III. Motions:

  1. Resolved: The Taiwanese government ought to increase its military spending by a significant amount.
